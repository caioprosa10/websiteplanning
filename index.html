Overview

This single-page site plan outlines the goals and design system for the proposed project. It includes site identity, purpose, audience scenarios, a color schema, typography choices, and low-fidelity wireframes for mobile and desktop layouts.

Note: The site plan is a living document and may evolve while implementing the project next week.

Naming Conventions

All files and folders referenced in this document follow the course naming rules: lowercase, kebab-case (hyphens), and no spaces or special characters.

  • Project folder: project/
  • HTML file: project/siteplan.html
  • Assets folders (optional for the final project): project/images/, project/styles/, project/scripts/

Tip: keep filenames short and descriptive, e.g., club-logo.svg, next-ride.jpg.

Site Name

Carioca eBiking Club

Why this name? It reflects a community club focused on electric biking in Rio de Janeiro ("Carioca"). The name is short, memorable, and clearly communicates the topic and locale.

  • Primary Identifier: Carioca eBiking Club (CEC)
  • Tagline: Ride farther. Climb easier. Explore Rio.
  • Optional domain: carioca-ebike.org (availability not required for this class)
Logo concept

A simple monogram CE set in Montserrat inside a rounded square using --brand as background and white foreground (as shown in the header).

Site Purpose

The site will serve as a hub for the Rio de Janeiro e-biking community by providing:

  • Trail Guides: curated e-bike friendly routes with elevation and surface notes.
  • Gear & Bike Advice: recommendations for hills, city commuting, and beach paths.
  • Events & Meetups: monthly group rides and safety workshops.
  • Membership Form: a simple form to join the club’s newsletter and rides.

Scenarios

  • “Which e-bike is best for Tijuca Forest climbs?” → Gear page compares torque, battery, and recommended models.
  • “Where can I see this month’s group ride schedule and route map?” → Events page with calendar and GPX download.
  • “How do I contact the organizers or become a member?” → Contact/Membership page with form and email details.

Color Schema

The site uses two primary colors and neutrals. This document demonstrates them live.

--brand #1E3A5F
Use: headings, header background, key CTAs
--accent #3B8EA5
Use: links, buttons, highlights
--bg #F6FAFD
Use: page background
--card #FFFFFF
Use: card and section backgrounds

Reference: How to Choose Good Website Color Schemes — websitebuilderexpert.com

Typography

Font Choices

  • Headings: Montserrat (600–800) — strong geometric sans for titles and navigation.
  • Body: Inter (400–600) — highly readable UI sans for paragraphs and UI.
  • Special Cases (optional): system monospace for code snippets when needed.

Reference: Choosing Web Fonts Beginners Guide — Google Design

Testing & Self‑Check

  • 1) Naming Conventions: Confirm all referenced paths use lowercase, kebab-case (e.g., project/siteplan.html).
  • 2) HTML Validation: Verify with the W3C HTML Validator (No errors). Warnings should be reviewed.
  • 3) CSS Validation: Validate this page’s CSS (copy the contents of the <style> block into the CSS validator) and ensure No errors.
  • 4) Purpose & Content: Site name, purpose, and at least two audience scenarios are present and clear.
  • 5) Color Schema: At least two colors are defined and used on this document (see variables under :root).
  • 6) Typography: Fonts listed (Montserrat, Inter) are actually used in headings/body here.
  • 7) Wireframes: Two sketches are present (mobile & desktop) for the Home page.
